INSURANCE CLAIM FULFILLMENT REQUIREMENTS

REQUIREMENT 1: USER MAIL
- Description: Valid contact email address
- Status: Automatically captured from email sender
- Validation: Must be valid email format
- Notes: Used for all communication regarding the claim

REQUIREMENT 2: REASON FOR CLAIM
- Description: Clear description of what happened
- Examples:
  * Vehicle accident details (date, location, circumstances)
  * Medical condition or injury description
  * Property damage explanation
  * Theft or loss incident details
- Required Details:
  * When the incident occurred
  * Where it happened
  * How it happened
  * What was affected/damaged

REQUIREMENT 3: CLAIM AMOUNT
- Description: Specific monetary amount being claimed
- Format: Dollar amount (e.g., $5,000, $12,500.50)
- Requirements:
  * Must be a specific number, not ranges
  * Should match supporting documentation
  * Include currency if applicable
- Examples: "$2,500 for medical bills", "$8,000 for vehicle repair"

REQUIREMENT 4: SUPPORTING PROOFS
- Description: Relevant documents and evidence
- Required Documents (depending on claim type):
  * Medical bills and reports
  * Repair estimates or invoices
  * Police reports (for accidents/theft)
  * Photos of damage
  * Receipts and proof of purchase
  * Insurance policy documents
- File Types Accepted:
  * Images: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif, .bmp
  * Documents: .pdf, .doc, .docx
  * Spreadsheets: .xls, .xlsx
- Quality Requirements:
  * Images must be clear and legible
  * Documents must be complete
  * All text must be readable
